WHAT IS THIS?

This website was built by Cécilemin and by PauIndeed. The project began in July 2026, as a way to collect, display, and archive every computerling for anyone to browse easily!

ABOUT THE DATABASE

The database's main objective is to gather all the computerlings, and to show all of them inside of a table. Said table shows:

  • The computerling's NAME
  • An IMAGE of the computerling.
  • The ACCOUNT the computerling appears on.
  • The OWNER(s) of the account(s)/computerling(s).
  • Their TYPING QUIRK (basically, how the computerling talks on interactions, for example, "🟡> Hi! I'm a puter! :D".)
  • And their STATUS - divided in 'Active', 'Semi-Active', 'In Hiatus' and 'Retired.'
    • (This can be subject to changes in the future for addition of other statuses.).

Ybu can petition for your own computerling to be added by filling out the petition form, and then going to the Céclings Discord server!
