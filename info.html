What is this website?

This website is a website built by Cécilemin and by PauIndeed, with the sole purpose of collecting and showing a database of all the possible computerlings possible!

The project began on July 2026, as a way to gather, collect, and easily show all the computerlings' accounts with their corresponding data and status, for anyone to see, and for anyone to search for computerlings inside of this database.

ㅤ

About the database

The database's main objective is to gather all the computerlings, and to show all of them inside of a table. Said table shows:

  • The NAME of the computerling
  • An IMAGE of said computerling
  • The twitter HANDLE of the computerling(s)
  • The OWNER(s) of the account(s)/computerling(s)
  • Their TYPING QUIRK (basically, how the computerling talks on interactions, for example, "🟡> Hi! I'm a puter! :D".)
  • And their STATUS - divided in 'Active', 'Semi-Active', 'In Hiatus' and 'Retired.'
    • (This can be subject to changes in the future for addition of other statuses.).

Anybody can access said database freely, as well as request their computerling to be included, by filling out the petition form and then requesting the introduction of their computerling in the Céclings discord server!
